# Product brief

## Vision

RoleReady is a personal career evidence system: a durable record of what someone actually did, what it proves, and how to communicate it credibly for the next opportunity.

## Primary user

An applicant who has valuable examples spread across old documents and memory, but finds it difficult to select, structure, and adapt them under the pressure of an application or interview. The experience is equally useful for experienced professionals, students, career changers, promotion candidates, and people returning to work.

## Product principles

1. **Evidence before eloquence.** The app may improve expression, never the underlying claim.
2. **Guided capture beats a blank page.** Every editor explains what useful evidence looks like.
3. **Show the reasoning.** Matches include factors, alternatives, cautions, and gaps.
4. **Prompts support speaking; scripts support writing.** Practice happens before an interview. RoleReady does not provide live interview assistance.
5. **Private by default.** The complete core experience works offline and without an account.
6. **Progress, not judgment.** Scores identify the next useful improvement.

## Core journey

```text
Capture evidence -> strengthen the story -> analyse a role -> inspect matches
-> create a grounded answer -> practise before the interview -> reflect afterwards
```

## Information architecture

- **Today** — preparation health, upcoming interviews, next best actions, and recent evidence.
- **Evidence** — searchable experience bank, guided capture, strength coaching, and source detail.
- **Roles** — opportunities, job analysis, requirements, match reports, gaps, and application status.
- **Practise** — confirmed answers, pre-interview practice decks, likely follow-ups, and confidence tracking.

Profile, capability Insights, Privacy, and Settings are secondary destinations opened from Today. This keeps the main navigation focused on the recurring career-preparation loop.

## Product boundaries

- RoleReady is local-first and account-free; there is no backend or cloud synchronisation.
- Generated text is deterministic and constrained to selected evidence. It is not an open-ended AI chatbot.
- Practice decks are designed for rehearsal before an interview, not covert or real-time interview assistance.
- Notifications are optional reminders for upcoming interviews only. Closing dates remain visible in the product but do not schedule notifications.
- JSON export is a user-readable, versioned data export. The current release does not restore or import an export.

## Success criteria

- A first useful evidence record can be captured in under four minutes.
- A pasted job description produces editable requirements and a transparent match report without a network call.
- No generated sentence adds a metric, employer, tool, action, or outcome absent from the selected evidence.
- A practice prompt is findable in two interactions and readable at large Dynamic Type sizes.
- Users can export or permanently delete their local data without contacting support.
