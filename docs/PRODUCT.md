# Product brief

## Vision

RoleReady is a personal career evidence system: a durable record of what someone actually did, what it proves, and how to communicate it credibly for the next opportunity.

## Primary user

An experienced applicant who has valuable examples spread across old documents and memory, but finds it difficult to select, structure, and adapt them under the pressure of an application or interview. The interaction stays equally useful for students, career changers, promotion candidates, and people returning to work.

## Product principles

1. Evidence before eloquence. The app may improve expression, never the underlying claim.
2. Guided capture beats a blank page. Every editor explains what good evidence looks like.
3. Show the reasoning. Matches include factors, alternatives, and gaps.
4. Prompts support speaking; scripts support writing. The default interview surface is concise.
5. Private by default. Core value works offline and without an account.
6. Progress, not judgment. Scores describe the next useful improvement.

## Information architecture

- **Today** — preparation health, upcoming dates, next best actions, and recent evidence.
- **Evidence** — searchable experience bank, guided capture, strength coaching, and source detail.
- **Roles** — opportunities, job analysis, match reports, gaps, and application status.
- **Prepare** — answer studio, interview packs, likely questions, and live interview mode.
- **You** — profile, capability insights, privacy, preferences, export, and data control.

## Success criteria

- A first useful evidence record can be captured in under four minutes.
- A pasted job description produces editable requirements and a transparent match report without a network call.
- No generated sentence adds a metric, employer, tool, action, or outcome absent from the selected evidence.
- An interview prompt is findable in two interactions and readable at large Dynamic Type sizes.
- Users can export or permanently delete their data without contacting support.
