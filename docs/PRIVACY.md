# Privacy summary

RoleReady processes career history, job descriptions, matching, and answer generation on the user’s device. It does not create an account, send content to a RoleReady server, sell data, or include advertising and analytics SDKs.

Users control four confidentiality levels per experience: public, private, confidential, and highly sensitive. Confidential and highly sensitive stories are visibly marked, can be excluded from matching, and are excluded from export by default. Export and full deletion are available in Settings.

If App Lock is enabled, authentication is performed by iOS. RoleReady receives only success or failure; Face ID, Touch ID, and passcode data remain under operating-system control.
